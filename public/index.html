Our Story

Born from the Ancient
Art of Perfumery

ATTARIK draws from centuries of fragrance tradition, blending the mystique of ancient attar-making with contemporary artistry to create scents that transcend time.

Every bottle in our collection is a testament to meticulous craftsmanship. We source the finest oud from Assam, the most delicate roses from Kannauj, and the purest sandalwood from Mysore — each ingredient chosen for its unparalleled character.

Our master perfumers, with decades of expertise, blend these precious materials into compositions that tell stories — of moonlit gardens, ancient temples, and timeless romance.

100% Authentic Pure ingredients, no synthetics

The Process

Artisan
Craftsmanship

Every ATTARIK fragrance passes through a meticulous journey from rare ingredient to bottled masterpiece.

01

Sourcing

We travel the world to handpick the rarest ingredients — Assam oud, Kannauj roses, Mysore sandalwood, and more.

02

Blending

Our master perfumers compose each fragrance through hundreds of trials, achieving the perfect harmony of notes.

03

Aging

Each blend is aged for weeks to months, allowing the ingredients to mature and reveal their true character and depth.

04

Bottling

Poured into handcrafted bottles, sealed with precision, and presented in luxury packaging worthy of the fragrance within.
